Return Policy

Our commitment to your satisfaction

Standard Figurines — Returns

We accept returns for standard (non-customized) figurines within 14 days of delivery, provided that:

  • The figurine is in its original, unused condition
  • It is returned in the original packaging
  • You provide your order number and reason for return

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the buyer, unless the item arrived damaged or defective.

Custom & Semi-Custom Figurines

Because custom and semi-custom figurines are made specifically for you, they cannot be returned for a refund. However, we are committed to your satisfaction:

  • We offer up to 2 rounds of revisions during the design approval process
  • If the finished figurine significantly differs from the approved design, we will work with you to make it right

Damaged or Defective Items

If your figurine arrives damaged or with a manufacturing defect, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with:

  • Your order number
  • Photos of the damage or defect
  • A description of the issue

We will arrange a free replacement or full refund for verified damaged/defective items.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method.
