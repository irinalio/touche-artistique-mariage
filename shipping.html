Shipping Policy

Everything you need to know about delivery of your figurines

Processing Times

Each figurine is handcrafted with care. Processing times vary depending on the type of order:

Order Type Processing Time
Standard Figurines 3-5 business days
Semi-Custom Figurines 1-2 weeks
Fully Custom Figurines 2-4 weeks

Delivery Times

Once your order has been processed and shipped, estimated delivery times are:

Destination Estimated Delivery
Europe 5-7 business days
United States & Canada 7-14 business days
Rest of the World 10-21 business days

Packaging & Protection

We understand that your figurine is a precious keepsake. Every order is:

  • Individually wrapped in protective foam
  • Placed in a custom-fitted gift box
  • Shipped in a sturdy outer carton to prevent damage

Tracking

A tracking number is provided for every order via email once your package has been shipped. You can follow your figurine's journey from our workshop to your doorstep.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and the size of your order. We occasionally offer free shipping promotions — follow us on Instagram and Pinterest to stay updated!
